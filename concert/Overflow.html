OVERFLOW

Performer Yilin Yang
Arrangement Mackie 802VLZ4 & Make Noise Strega
Duration 10'
Year 2025

Overflow is an improvised electronic performance that unfolds not through conscious intention, but through the intuitive guidance of my senses. Rather than being dictated by structured thought, the piece emerges organically, shaped by the resonance of touch, sound and sight.

Each interaction - be it the tactile resistance of the synthesizer's knobs, the subtle shifts in sonic feedback, or the evolving textures of layered frequencies - drives the performance forward, creating a dialogue between body and machine.

As the music evolves, my emotions rise and swell, accumulating in waves until they overflow the boundaries of conscious control, spilling over into an unfiltered sonic presence. This state of surrender allows the sound to exist in its purest form - ephemeral, fleeting, yet deeply immersive.

Inspired by Aldous Huxley's The Doors of Perception, I seek to capture "The miracle, moment by moment, of naked existence". The piece is an invitation to suspend judgment and simply inhabit the sound, embracing the immediacy of its unfolding.

Using a modular synthesizer and a no-input mixer, I explore the delicate balance between control and chaos. The no-input mixer, which generates feedback loops as a self-contained sound source, introduces an unpredictable element that shapes the piece in real time. The interaction between these two instruments creates a dynamic sonic landscape - one that is constantly shifting, resisting structure and existing purely in the moment. Overflow is not just a composition, but a process - an evolving exchange between impulse and response, where sound and self merge, dissolve and reform in an ever-shifting sonic current.
